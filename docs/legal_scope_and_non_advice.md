# Legal Scope and Non-Advice Statement

LEXON is a research prototype for executable legal-information representation and conformance testing.

## LEXON does not:

- provide legal advice;
- determine legal compliance;
- certify conformity with the EU AI Act or any other legal instrument;
- replace legal interpretation by qualified professionals;
- validate whether a system substantively satisfies a legal obligation.

## LEXON outputs concern:

- formal applicability under encoded assumptions;
- evidentiary completeness relative to encoded evidence specifications;
- direct conflicts under an explicit incompatibility vocabulary;
- candidate evidence-remediation items.

## All outputs require human review.

The synthetic conformance suite does not constitute an expert-validated legal corpus. Conformance suite results verify implementation of the stated formal semantics; they do not establish external legal validity.
