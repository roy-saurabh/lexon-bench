# Revision from MDPI Information Submission to Artificial Intelligence and Law Submission

The v1.1.0 artifact revises the v1.0.2 MDPI Information submission artifact for an *Artificial Intelligence and Law* (Springer) submission.

The v1.0.2 artifact is archived at DOI 10.5281/zenodo.20399201 and is preserved in the git history of this repository.

## Main changes

1. Reframed LEXON from a broad information-systems contribution to an AI & Law executable legal-reasoning artifact.
2. Renamed the synthetic benchmark as a deterministic conformance suite.
3. Added explicit AI & Law positioning (`docs/ai_law_positioning.md`).
4. Added non-advice and legal-scope documentation (`docs/legal_scope_and_non_advice.md`).
5. Added an illustrative EU AI Act mapping (`data/illustrative/`, `docs/eu_ai_act_illustrative_mapping.md`).
6. Added derivation-trace examples (`outputs/traces/`, `docs/trace_format.md`).
7. Added formal-property tests (`tests/test_formal_properties.py`).
8. Added annotation rubric (`docs/annotation_rubric.md`).
9. Clarified that the synthetic conformance suite is not an expert-validated legal corpus.
10. Preserved v1.0.2 DOI history in all metadata files and documentation.
11. Updated software version to 1.1.0 in all metadata files.
12. Updated preferred citation to *Artificial Intelligence and Law* / Springer.
